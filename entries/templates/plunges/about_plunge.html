All credit for the Plunge Meditation goes to Edrid from www.sandoth.com.

We approach a one-minute meditation as a "plunge." You plunge into it. You cast away, let go of, and drop everything. You dive in. Your focus is 100% on your topic. You become completely open to the truth that your topic points to. You allow for complete absorption in your experience of the topic.. There is a list of topics. You can add your own topics as you wish, but the ones supplied here are pretty good. My advice: do just one, then let it rest. Let hours go by before you do another.
